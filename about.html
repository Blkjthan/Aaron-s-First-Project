ILoop is a company that promotes Computer gaming equipment and software. It was founded by Aaron Wright and his son Ethan in 2025. Aaron has been a gamer since 1990 and his son since 2017. They share their knowledge of the most current gaming hardware and software with anyone beginning to play games and persons involved in the world of gaming.

Aaron Wright, the Founder of ILoop and his sons Ethan Wright, Kingson Pierrot and Noah Nash are gamers willing to share their knowledge of many games and will assist anyone starting to game with the appropriate hardware and software to begin their journey into gaming.
